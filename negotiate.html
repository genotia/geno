Why negotiating online works

People negotiate better when they're not face-to-face. We built the science into software.

🧠

Different mental model, different entry point

Browsing deals is passive — scroll and pick. Negotiating is active — you set a goal and an agent works for you. These are two completely different user intents that should never compete for attention in the same menu.

🔄

The live feed is the retention hook

Showing active negotiations with live status, round count, and savings creates a reason to open OffPeak even when you're not starting something new. It's your engagement loop.

🌍

Global positioning from day one

"Negotiate" is universally understood across every market — India, UAE, Singapore, UK. It needs no translation or cultural adaptation. A feature this powerful deserves its own top-level tab.
