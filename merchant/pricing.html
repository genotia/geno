Frequently asked questions

Still have questions? Email us and we’ll reply within a few hours.

No hidden costs. Your subscription covers unlimited bookings, all core features, and the free communication allowance. The only additional charges are pay-as-you-go communication fees when you exceed your monthly allowance — and these are shown transparently on this page. Automated transactional emails (booking confirmations, receipts) are always free on every plan with no cap.
Genoti Negotiate is our most differentiated feature — no competitor offers AI-powered deal negotiation. We want every merchant on every plan to benefit from it from day one, and for every consumer to associate Genoti with smarter deals. It’s our edge and the reason merchants recommend us to other merchants.
Each plan includes a free monthly WhatsApp allowance for automated notifications — booking confirmations, reminders, and deal alerts. Once you exceed that allowance, you pay ₹25 per 100 additional messages. Marketing campaigns and AI negotiation messages have separate rates as shown in the communication pricing section above. All WhatsApp rates include Meta’s platform fee with no markup.
At the end of your trial, choose a plan and add payment details. Your account is never charged automatically during the trial — there are no surprise charges. If you don’t pick a plan, your account pauses and your data is safely stored for 30 days.
Yes. Upgrade anytime — new features unlock immediately and you’re billed the prorated difference. Downgrade at the end of your billing cycle. No penalties and no long-term contracts on any plan.
No commission on bookings, ever. Unlike competitors who charge 15–20% per new client from their marketplace, Genoti charges a flat subscription. You keep 100% of every transaction. The Genoti Negotiate success fee (₹29–₹149 per successfully negotiated deal) is paid by the consumer, not the merchant.
All prices shown are exclusive of GST (18%). GST will be added at checkout. If you are a GST-registered business, you can claim the input tax credit on your Genoti subscription — effectively reducing your net cost by 18%.
